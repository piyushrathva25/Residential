Shiv 1 is a thoughtfully planned residential housing society located in Jitodiya, Anand, Gujarat. The project is designed to provide a comfortable, secure, and well-organized living environment, making it ideal for families seeking peaceful residential surroundings with good connectivity.

Project Overview

Shiv 1 Society features well-designed residential units developed with a focus on structural quality, practical layouts, and long-term durability. The society offers a balanced combination of modern construction standards and everyday livability.

Key Highlights

  • Located in a growing residential area of Jitodiya, Anand
  • Strong RCC construction ensuring safety and stability
  • Well-planned internal layout with proper road access
  • Adequate natural light and ventilation in residential units
  • Suitable for family living and long-term investment
